What is Hotel Mystery Shopping?
Hotel mystery shopping is a survey done for the hotel industry to help check the areas for improvement in customer satisfaction. This is done by a mystery shopper auditor, who acts like a customer to get information from employees and other existing customers about the services and experience in the hotel. It starts with checking in, facilities, employee service to the customer, guest room quality, and checking out procedures. This observed process by the mystery shopper gives you valuable insight into the area that needs improvement.

The mystery shopper then submits detailed mystery shopping feedback about his/her experience. This survey helps you identify and address issues within the hotel industry, such as the improvement in staff training, customer service standards, and operational efficiency, that will better increase customer satisfaction and loyalty.
